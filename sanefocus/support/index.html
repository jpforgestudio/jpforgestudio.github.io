Common questions

Does SaneFocus need Accessibility permission?

No. SaneFocus restores focus by asking macOS to reactivate the app you were using before quick capture. It does not need Accessibility permission for this behavior.

Where are notes stored?

Notes are stored locally on your Mac. SaneFocus does not require an account and does not upload your notes or analytics.

How do I report a bug?

Send an email with your macOS version, SaneFocus version, what happened, and the steps that reproduce the issue if you know them.

Can I request a feature?

Yes. Feature requests are welcome. A short description of the workflow you are trying to improve is especially helpful.
