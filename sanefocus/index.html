SaneFocus for macOS

Catch the thought. Keep the focus.

A lightweight macOS scratchpad for quick notes, keyboard-first TODOs, and getting back to what you were doing.

See features Support

Small on purpose.

SaneFocus is not a workspace. It is a fast place to put the thought down, organize it lightly, and return to the work in front of you.

Quick capture

Open and hide notes instantly with global shortcuts or the menu bar.

Keyboard-first

Manage lightweight TODO states, duplicate lines, indent notes, and move completed items down.

Lightweight tabs

Keep separate thought streams in compact tabs without opening a full notes workspace.

Local by default

Notes stay local on your Mac. SaneFocus does not require an account or upload analytics.

Made to stay out of the way.

Pin notes while working, switch sides, adjust text size, and use simple Markdown styling when a note needs a little structure.
