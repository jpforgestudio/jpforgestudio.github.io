SaneFocus for macOS

Catch the thought.
Return to flow.

A lightweight macOS scratchpad for quick notes, keyboard-first TODOs, and returning to the app you were using.

Get on the Mac App Store See features

Small on purpose.

SaneFocus is not a workspace. It is a fast place to put the thought down, organize it lightly, and return to the work in front of you.

Quick capture

Open and hide notes instantly with global shortcuts or the menu bar.

Keyboard-first

Manage lightweight TODO states, duplicate lines, indent notes, and move completed items down.

Five quiet tabs

Keep separate thought streams in five compact tabs. Titles update from what you write, so context stays recognizable without extra setup.

Local by default

Notes stay local on your Mac. SaneFocus does not require an account, Accessibility permission, or analytics upload.

Open it. Write it down. Return to work.

Keep notes pinned while working, switch sides when the window is in the way, and use simple Markdown when a thought needs structure.
